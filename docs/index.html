VertX Game Engine

A brand new game engine developed by 3 students!

Context

Welcome to the index of VertX Game Engine, an innovative creation by three talented students from CITM-UPC University in Barcelona, Spain. Developed during the Game Engines course of the 2023-24 academic year. VertX is the result of four months of dedication, passion, and collaboration. Crafted within the robust framework of C++, this game engine leverages the power of OpenGL for cutting-edge graphics rendering, ensuring a seamless and immersive gaming experience. Our journey has been enriched by the incorporation of various open-source libraries, reflecting our commitment to community-driven development. Join us in exploring the possibilities of VertX Game Engine and witness the culmination of our academic endeavors transform into a platform that inspires creativity and empowers game developers.
